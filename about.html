Our Story

Shri Anant Anath Ashram Nathana Bathinda was founded in 2008 by Saint Baba Ramanand Ji. His vision was to create a nurturing home for children in need, where they can grow with love, care, and education.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a safe, spiritual, and educational environment to children, enabling them to lead fulfilling lives. Our organization runs solely on the generosity of donors and well-wishers.
